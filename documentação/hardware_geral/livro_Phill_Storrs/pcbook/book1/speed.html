  • The Core Clock Speed is the internal operating speed of the processor.
  • The Bus speed is the clock speed at which the processor addresses it's memory.
  • The PCI Bus Speed is the operating speed of the external PCI Bus slots
  • The multiplier is the multiplication factor between the Bus Speed and the Core Speed.
  • 3.3 and 3.5 means the processor can operate on either voltage
  • 2.8 & 3.3 means the processor needs two supply voltages

Pentium Pro Processors use a Socket 8 and Pentium II Processors a Slot 1 connector. Socket 8 and Slot 1, have faster Data Buses to provide faster access to the Level 2 cache. Slot 1 is electrically identical to Socket 8 but uses an edge connector rather than a Pin Grid type socket.
